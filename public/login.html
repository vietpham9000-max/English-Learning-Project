How Practicing Dictation & Shadowing Improves Your English Skills

When practicing exercises, you will go through 4 main steps, all of them are equally important!

1. Listen to the audio

Listen carefully — it’s the key to improving your listening skills.

2. Type what you hear

Typing forces you to focus on details: spelling, pronunciation, and writing.

3. Check & correct

Learn from mistakes to improve accuracy and comprehension.

4. Read it out loud

Reading out loud helps your pronunciation and speaking skills.
